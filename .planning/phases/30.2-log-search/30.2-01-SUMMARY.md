- Match navigation (Enter/Shift+Enter, prev/next buttons) with auto-scroll via Virtuoso

## Decisions Made
- Separate search store from existing log-store filter — search highlights in-place while filter hides entries, both coexist independently
- Amber/gold highlight colors (rgba 245,158,11) with subtle opacity for Liquid Glass aesthetic — active match gets stronger highlight with box-shadow ring
- Match computation runs in useEffect on entries/query change — simple iteration over filtered+reversed array, no additional debounce needed for 5K buffer
