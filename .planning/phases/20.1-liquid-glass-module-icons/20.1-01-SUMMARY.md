## Summary

Redesigned all 15 module icons in `module-icons.tsx` with Apple Liquid Glass SVG technique — replacing flat stroke-only icons with translucent gradient fills, inner highlights, and soft glowing edges.

## What Changed

### packages/dashboard/src/components/icons/module-icons.tsx
- Added `<defs>` blocks to each icon with two `<linearGradient>` elements:
  - Glass gradient: accent color from ~40% opacity (top) to ~12% opacity (bottom) — translucent colored glass body
  - Highlight gradient: white from ~25% opacity (top) to 0% (bottom) — frosted inner glow
- Primary shapes filled with glass gradient + subtle strokes at 60% opacity (down from 1.5 to 1 strokeWidth)
- Highlight overlays as inset shapes filled with white gradient for specular reflection
- Select icons got additional subtle white stroke paths for extra glass refraction detail (LocationIcon, PushIcon, CrashLogsIcon, FsIcon, ToolSettingsIcon, DevUtilsIcon)
- Secondary/detail elements retain lighter opacity treatment
- All 15 icons: DevicesIcon, LocationIcon, AppsIcon, LogsIcon, ScreenshotIcon, DeepLinksIcon, PushIcon, FsIcon, DatabaseIcon, SettingsIcon, CrashLogsIcon, MediaIcon, ClipboardIcon, ToolSettingsIcon, DevUtilsIcon

### Preserved (unchanged)
- `IconProps` interface
- `moduleIconMap` export
- `moduleLabelMap` export
- All component function names
- All accent colors per icon
